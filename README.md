### RISK FACTORS LEADING TO CHRONIC HEART DISEASE
- Elevated cholesterol levels
- Pre-existing congenital heart disease
- Existence of diabetes in an individual.
- Hypertension
- Obesity
- Sedentary life
- Smoking
- Unhealthy diet
